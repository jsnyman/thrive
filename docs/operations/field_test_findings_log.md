## Severity Guidance

- `Critical`: blocks trading, risks data loss, or breaks end-of-day recovery
- `High`: causes incorrect operational decisions, repeated workflow failure, or unresolved sync risk
- `Medium`: creates staff confusion, slows work materially, or requires workaround
- `Low`: cosmetic, intermittent, or minor documentation/training issue
